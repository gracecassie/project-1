ABOUT US

The Purple Sloth opened its doors in June 2019. Our owners, Grace and Cassie, decided on it's name after backpacking trip to beautiful Costa Rica, where they resided in a sloth sanctuary for a period of time, learning about and taking care of various species of sloths. After coming back home, they wanted to carry on the legacy of the sloth by providing a chill and relaxing space to safely drink in the historic part of Uptown New Orleans.

Enjoy our outdoor and indoor areas decorated with cozy lounge chair, fire pits, and hammocks so you can hang like a sloth!
